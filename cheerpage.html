Cheerleading

Emma Szwast

This was my first year participating in cheer, and I am very happy to have recently made both football and basketball squads for next year. I am very grateful to my best friend who convinced me to go out for the team because I definitely wouldn't have done it otherwise. My favorite part about cheerleading is getting to be a part of such an amazing squad who is always there for each other.
